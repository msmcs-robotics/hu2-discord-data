Lesson 4 - Creating your first listing In order to create your first listing, you will first need to understand if the item is already available for sale on Amazon. If you are private label, the item will NOT be available as you are the creator of that branded product and will need to create a full new listing. If you are selling wholesale or retail arbitrage then it is likely your product is already on Amazon, you will just need to select what condition the item is in. You will just need to select some options including; - Condition - Your price - amount of units for sale - Who will fulfil the order (Amazon or merchant)

Lesson 6 - Understanding profit margins ‚ÄòBusiness is a game of margins, not volume‚Äô This is a critical step to selecting products for generating a healthy profit (important for investors) A good EBITDA (profit before Tax) on Amazon should be between 20 - 35% When researching products I always advocate that you should look for a product that can sell for 3-5x the unit cost for example; If you buy at $5, you should be able to sell for a LOW price of $15, but have a retail price of $20-25 Now you may be thinking ‚Äòthat sounds great‚Äô - No, you are missing many fees, let me break them down; Purchase - $5 Shipping to Amazon - $0.5 Referal fee - $3.25 Fulfilment fee - $3.50 Advertising cost - $4 Total fees - $16.25 Sale price - $25 Profit - $8.75 This is ignoring the professional seller subscription fee So you see, we are roughly at 34% profit.
