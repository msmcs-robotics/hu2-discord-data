MANDATORY READING Part of the learning process here is asking questions. However, a lot of you don't know how to ask questions properly, and I'm going to teach you how. A very common form of question here is "Thoughts on <topic> ?" or "Should I <action>?" These aren't real questions, they are too broad and the one reading them can't tell which answer do you want/need due to lack of information. The formula for a good question: 1- Be as specific as possible 2- Let us know your exact situation 3- Tell us what YOU THINK you should be doing and what you have ALREADY TRIED to solve your situation AND to get information You must become an articulate person if you wish to succeed. You must ask questions as clearly as possible to get the best answer which you seek. "Thoughts on XRP?" isn't a question. "How's the weather?" isn't a question.
